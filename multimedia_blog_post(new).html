How to start filming a vlog!

First, you need to thing about what will you do in the vlog

By watching some samples on the internet like: Instagram, Youtube, TikTok,... You will find a great source of ideas and angles to set your camera. And also how they edit the videos too, what fonts they used, what colors they picked for the videos.

List the activities in order so that you can easily manage you scenes

Some can also read some examples that some others vloggers have done before here.

Then, just start filming your own videos!

Some important things you need to focus on your video!

Cameras' angles

The most essential factor you have to calculate very carefully is your cameras' angles

The way you move yours cameras decide how your videos look like, which could effects the viewers thinking of you.

If your camera's too high, it will make all the objects look lower and bigger (most suitable for foods or cosmestic products). For filming daily vlog, just use normal way to film your videos if it horizontal, if it a vertical video, the camera should be uder your head.

Music

The audio also decide the atmosphere, the vibe for your videos

Rock is suitable for motivated video! Like studying vlogs or contests vlogs.

Soft music help viewer enjoy your work easier! For daily vlogs or unboxing videos

Usually, you should use instrucmental music to avoid copyright for your videos!
